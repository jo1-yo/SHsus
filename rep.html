Reptile Club
Focused on understanding and appreciating the small creatures that keep ecosystems balanced, this club used specimen preparation to highlight the importance of biodiversity.

Participants made butterfly specimens, choosing from species like the Blue Nawab. As they worked, fun facts were shared, such as butterflies pollinating 75% of food crops. A "Biodiversity Bingo" game followed, where spotting traits in specimens earned a mini field guide to local insects.
