Selda Geography Club
Dedicated to addressing the issue of birds colliding with urban buildings, the "Anti-Bird-Collision Workshop" started with a talk on the high number of bird deaths from window collisions. Attendees then used craft supplies to design "bird-safe window art," from UV-patterned stickers to hanging mobiles. The best designs were displayed on campus windows, with plans to track collision reductions. Participants also took home a "Bird Friend" checklist for spotting local species.
