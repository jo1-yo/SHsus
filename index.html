About Our Initiative

Bringing the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) into campus, this is a student-led initiative promoting sustainable actions in Shanghai. By deeply exploring each of the 17 themes, more than 30 student organizations, clubs and NGOs have engaged in long-term projects including practical workshops, debates, charity sales, exhibitions and tech innovations. This initiative empowers students to connect their original ideas with global sustainability efforts, transforming abstract goals into concrete action to make a difference to our society. Through this hands-on approach, we aim to cultivate global awareness, creative problem-solving, and a strong sense of social responsibility among youth.
