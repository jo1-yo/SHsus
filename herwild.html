Herwild

Herwild is a student-led non for-profit organization that capitalizes women's intangible cultural heritages and handicrafts with creative eco-friendly re-design techniques and branding through story-telling videos in rural Chinese villages. We have Sold to 6 enterprises as 2025 Women's Day welfare and raised $10k+ revenues with a net profit margin of 17.5% for underprivileged women. We have reinvested the profits to host handicrafts' workshop in several primary schools in Shanghai.

The gift box is more than just a source of income for women in Anhui village. It has transformed a collection of isolated workshops into a connected community. Through these delicate handicrafts, we are not only aiming to alleviate women out of poverty but also present the rich culture of our hometown to the modern world.
