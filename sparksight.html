Sparksight is dedicated to creating diverse cultures, uncovering the flashes of thinking that cannot be judged by standardized assessment, and sharing the profound insights and unrestrained imagination of young people with everyone from a unique perspective. The theme is SDG sustainable development. Through this theatrical speech event, sustainable development could be brought into the campus and the sustainable development of society, economy and environment can be practiced in innovation and creativity. Six speakers focused on the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and engaged in their own thoughts on topics such as gender equality, hunger, poverty, and social equity.
