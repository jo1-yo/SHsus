EcoQuest Board Game Club
The club turned sustainability concepts into several engaging board games. The goal is to make SDGs and B Corp “business for good” ideas standout through interactive gameplay. Participants paired up as company leaders, using action cards to make decisions that balanced profit and shared benefits. A “climate crisis wildcard” added excitement, with unexpected events like resource shortages or eco-protests shaking up strategies. The winning team took home a “Sustainability Champion” badge, and everyone got to dive into real B Corp success stories, sparking interesting discussions about what true business success entails.
